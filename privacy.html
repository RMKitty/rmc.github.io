Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

In Short: ZenFocus respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All data is kept locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Locally Stored Data

ZenFocus stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Focus session records (duration, activity type, date)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Statistics and progress data

Important: This data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

1.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

2. How We Use Your Information

Data stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Display your focus session history
  • Calculate statistics and progress
  • Save your app preference settings
  • Provide a personalized focus experience

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All data is securely stored on your device using SQLite database. This data is protected by your device's operating system security measures.

3.2 Data Transmission

Since we do not collect or transmit any data to external servers, there is no risk of data transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Audio Content

ZenFocus uses ambient sound effects from Pixabay.com under the Pixabay License. These audio files are included in the app and do not require external connections. Attribution is not required but is appreciated.

4.2 No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, or tracking SDKs.

5. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Access: You can view all data in the app at any time
  • Delete: You can delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Export: Data is stored in your device's local database
  • Control: You can modify or delete any session record at any time

6. Children's Privacy

ZenFocus is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no children's privacy concerns.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through app updates, and the new policy will be available in the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: zenfocuslse@gmail.com
  • In-App Feedback: Settings > Contact Us

9. Consent

By using ZenFocus, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
